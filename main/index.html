How much does it cost? +

Sessions are $80 for Kindy to Year 6 and $90 for Year 7 to 12. Specialised HSC subjects are $100 a lesson. Every session is one-on-one, in your home, with a qualified tutor.

Where do you tutor? +

In your own home, right across the Greater Sydney region. Your tutor comes to you, so there is no travel and no pick-ups.

What year levels and subjects? +

Pre-Kindy through to Year 12 and the HSC, in Maths and Literacy and Reading, all aligned to the current NSW syllabus.

How are tutors matched to my child? +

We match on your child's year level, the subjects they need, and your location, then confirm the details with you within 48 hours of enrolling.
